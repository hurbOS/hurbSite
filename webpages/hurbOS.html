HurbOS is going to be a lightweight and stable *nix-like operating system programmed in C. It was started on October 29th of 2019, and is still being developed to this day. It's on GitHub here. At the moment, it is just a terminal that says "Welcome to hurbOS" and nothing else. Our ultimate goal is to create A Unix like OS with security and stability in mind.

Roadmap:

☑ Get a bootable helloworld OS (On its own branch)
❎ Get some standard headers
❎ Bash? zsh!
❎ ZFS
❎ Installer
❎ Package Manager (LIGHT)
❎ More hurb flavoured software!
