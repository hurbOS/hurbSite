What is hurbIndustries?

We're a growing group of programmers making software such as hurbOS, LIGHT, and hurbIM. We do not do this for profit, but are simply a group of people who enjoy writing programs together. We were founded on October 29th of 2019, by Thou and CaptainJamason.

FAQ

Is hurbIndustries a meme?

While hurbIndustries started out completely as a meme, it's now slightly less of a meme since we have actual projects going on.

Why "hurb?"

"hurb" is "bruh" backwards, we thought it was funny.

How can I contribute to hurb projects?

It's simple! Just make a push request to one of our projects github pages.

Is hurb software currently usable?

The short answer is no. We're at an extremely early stage in development of all of our software, and they're currently unable to be used right now. However, we are planning to get something usable as soon as possible.
